About ANABIA


ANABIA is creating the supply chain of the future. Our vision is to make life better for
one billion people in the supply chain.


We are fast growing company established in September 2013 with 2 employees, today in 2016 with 62 employees.
Our head office is located in Dhaka, for the convenience of our clients, near to the airport. To be closer to our
businesspartners, be able to act fast and control the development of the products,

we created the network of representative and sourcing offices in
Dubai (UAE), Karachi (Pakistan), Colombo (Sri Lanka), and Mumbai (India)
